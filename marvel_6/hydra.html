Hydra

    

This is just a couple of Hydra henchmen that I made to go along with my Viper custom. They were made from Daredevil movie figures. The H logo was made from toothpaste tube and the sub-machine guns and pistols were just random leftover accessories.

I thought for a bit that it might be cool to make a small army of these relatively easy customs, but then sanity prevailed. I mean, a Hydra lackey is already pretty lame. Why should I make more of them?
