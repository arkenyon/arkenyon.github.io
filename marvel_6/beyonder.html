The Beyonder

    

He hails from...beyond. He "beyonds" more than any other. He is The Beyonder!

Relatively simple recipe: 2000 AD Johnny Alpha head, McFarlane Akira body, DCD Hourman arms. I was inspired by this head, which to me looks just like the Beyonder. I cut some of the straps off of the costume, for fear of a resemblance to Michael Jackson. I tried to match the "Malibu tan" of the face on the arms. Probably one of the very few customs I will make that looks something like a regular person.

Hopefully I will have a better quality pic available soon.
