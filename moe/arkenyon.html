Black Knight

Recipe:
Warlands body with Power Rangers Knasty Knight upper head and Medieval Spawn 1 lower head.

Comments:
This custom is based mostly on the Heroes Reborn version of the Black Knight with some artistic license on the head. The original Black Knight costume and "scuba mask" helmet were just too goofy to do.

Crimson Cowl II

Recipe:
Crimson Lisseth, Fembot arms, DC Direct Spectre cape.

Comments:
I sculpted the cowl out of Magic sculpt and did lots of drybrushing. The figure has a whopping count of 4 points of articulation if you don't count the flexible cape.

Radioactive Man

Recipe:
ToyBiz Giant figure with Ripped and Ruthless Stone Cold head.

Comments:
I used flourescent (neon) paints for the skintones. A black light makes the figure glow. This picture was taken in partial black light and natural light for a subdued glow.

Ultron 5

Recipe:
Marvel Legends Iron Man torso, feet, and gloves; Marvel Legends Captain America legs and arms; modified ToyBiz Vault Ultron head; Beetle shins.

Comments:
I made casts of either side of the Ultron head put them together with a little extra spacing to widen the head. Limb swapping on the two Marvel Legends figures was an unexpected nightmare, and did not go too well. The figure was painted with silver spray paint and Tamiya translucent blue, then gloss-coated.

Tiger Shark

Recipe:
Goldberg body with Despero head. Vinyl fins.

Comments:
This was one of my favorite villains from way back. Mostly a nostalgia custom.
