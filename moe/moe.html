In reaction to the waves of new Marvel figures arriving at retail stores, a rogue group of sculptors calling themselves the Sinister Six has unleashed a mongrel horde of villains in the form of the Avengers' nemeses, Masters of Evil. No less than thirty figures make up the line, representing the better part of seven different incarnations of the Masters of Evil. Unfortunately, due to technical difficulties, three figures (Yellowjacket II, Constrictor, and Baron Zemo I) were not available in time for the show. They will be shown in the near future.
