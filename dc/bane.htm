Bane

Although I like the B&R Bane figure, I just didn't think it was big and imposing enough, especially standing next to TJ Batman. So, I made a bigger, badder Bane.

The head is from B&R Bane, the torso from MF Bishop, and the legs from some Ghost Rider figure. I painted viens under the flesh-colored coat of paint to emphasize the muscularity and venom effect.
