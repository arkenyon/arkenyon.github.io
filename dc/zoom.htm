The Reverse Flash

The Reverse Flash is basically a Daredevil repaint, with ear-wing thingies from a TJ Flash. I had thought about using this base for a while but I wasn't sure if it would work. The Hancock's Flash removed all doubt. Also, Leslie Hancock's decoupage article was very helpful in making and applying the chest emblem.

The figure is a little tall to be compatible with Total Justice figures, but with the ball-jointed legs I can "superpose" him to make him fit in.
