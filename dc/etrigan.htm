Etrigan the Demon

Etrigan was the first custom figure that I finished. He was constructed from a ToyBiz Water Wars Wolverine body and a Hercules (TV series) Mesomorph head and arms. The really cool thing about this custom is that both base figures had the rotating forearms feature, and I was able to incorporate this articulation into Etrigan. He is intended to fit in with my Total Justice figures.
