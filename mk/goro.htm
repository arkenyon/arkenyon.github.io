Goro

This is my most complex cutsom to date. It took about six months on and off to finish, but I'm glad I saw it through. Here are all of the parts and pieces that paid the ultimate sacrifice.

  • Head, torso, upper arms = Onslaught Wolverine (lots of dremeling)
  • Lower arms = Leader Hulk
  • Hands = epoxy putty, Magisculp
  • Feet = Abomination
  • Leg wrappings = rubber bands
  • Bracelets = inner tube pieces
  • Belt buckle = metal button snap
  • Sash = Mummies Alive
  • Ponytail = don't remember
