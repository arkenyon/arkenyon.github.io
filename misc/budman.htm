Budman

Budman was a creation spawned from idle time and distorted values stemming from a college dorm environment, circa 1988. He was constructed from a variety of brands of beer cans, held together with super glue. 

That's me (10 years and 10 pounds ago) thrown in for scale. 

 

Budman was only a sculpture, but he did have one action feature - complete and utter self-destruction. The pictures below tell the sordid tale of Budman's final hour.

The end. 

Let us have a brief moment of silence to remember Budman. He may have only been a pile of cans; but to those who knew him, he was more like a neatly stacked pile of cans that was glued together. 

Please note that all cans were cleaned up and properly recycled, and that no animals were harmed in the discourse of this event. Budman was simply intended to be a novelty item, not an endorsement of alchohol.
