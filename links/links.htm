Reference Information:

  • Raving Toy Maniac THE be-all, end-all action figure site. These guys set the standard, and they aren't even selling anything!

  • RTM Custom Corner My favorite section of the Raving Toy Maniac site. It contains custom galleries, recipes, articles, and links (check out my Figure Scale Study posted here). The CC e-mail listserve provides group therapy for those of us afflicted with Figyer-Hackre's Syndrome.

  • Another Universe Be sure to check out Chris Galdieri's Scratchbuilders Toolbox (and archives) for great customizing ideas. He also writes interesting columns spotlighting different action figures. Talk about a dream job!

  • Gremlins in the Garage A very cool site dedicated to figure models. The gallery section has a huge collection of patrons' finished models. Just about every movie monster you can imagine is here.
